## Linking Best Practices

1. **Be Subtle**: Instead of saying "Check out our HMS," say "Clinics running a [connected HMS](/products/hospital-management-system) often see..."
2. **Contextual Anchors**: Link phrases that describe the *benefit* rather than the product name.
3. **Cross-Pollination**: Refer back to the [origin story](/blogs/why-hospital-pharmacies-fail) when talking about patient access or mission.
